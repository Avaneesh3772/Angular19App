netSalary = computed(() => gross() - tax() - deductions()) — computed() is read-only and lazy: it only recalculates when one of the signals it reads has changed. Angular caches the result until then.

computed() vs signal(): You cannot call .set() or .update() on a computed signal — it is always derived. If you need a signal that is derived but can also be manually overridden, use linkedSignal() instead.
